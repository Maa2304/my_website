About

“Perhaps what you insist on is destined for you”

The Computer Information Systems (CIS) major is one of the most important majors in information and computer technology. This major combines various computer science majors and organizational and administrative fields, where the student acquires the knowledge and skills necessary to analyze, design, develop and operate information systems, and benefit from them in any public or private institutions.Currently, the department contains 24 faculty members, including 16 members who hold doctoral degrees, and 8 members who hold master’s degrees. Hundreds of students at various university levels graduate from the CIS Department.
